Steak

Description

Ingredients

  • 300g of desired steak part
  • 50g of butter
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • A few branches of thyme
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Let the steak be at room temperature
  2. Season the steak well with salt and pepper based on taste
  3. Get an oiled skillet and heat it until smoking hot
  4. Add the steak in facing away from yourself. For medium rare, each side should be cooked for 2-3 minutes
  5. Add crushed garlic and thyme
  6. Serve steak on a dish and let it sit for a few minutes
