Egg Fried Rice

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 300g of left-over rice
  • 1 Onion
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Crack two eggs into a bowl and beat it up finely
  2. Add rice into the bowl
  3. Heat up an oiled pan with medium heat
  4. Diced up the onion and stir fry until golden brown
  5. Add rice mixture into the pan and use salt and paper based on your tastes
  6. Serve in a small bowl after rice is fully coocked
